Dr. Revathi Selwyn

Dr. Revathi Selwyn is a family practitioner and an author. She lives in Hyderabad with her husband and has two grown children. She loves nature, birds and animals. Her favourite pets are her two Labrador retrievers, Amy and Teddy. She loves writing and her books are fun to read, and inspire children to be kind to one another and to animals and nature. The "Created for a Purpose" series of stories are about a brother and sister who learn through nature that God has a purpose for everything He creates.
