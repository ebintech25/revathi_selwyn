Josh-Posh, Little Hug-Me & The Avocado

This is one of the stories from "Created for a Purpose" series of books. The story revolves around Josh-Posh and his sister, Little Hug-Me and the avocado seed that they plant. Birds nest within the plant and lay eggs. The eggs hatch into chicks. The children find out that everything God creates has a purpose.

Josh-Posh, Little Hug-Me & Izzy-Busy

This is one of the stories from, "Created for a Purpose" series of books. Josh-Posh and his sister, Little Hug-Me visit their grand parents, and have fun with Izzy-Busy, Booster the Rooster and other animals on the country side. The children learn that everything that God creates has a purpose.

Josh-Posh, Little Hug-Me & Pepe the Spotted Dove

This is one of the stories from, “Created for a Purpose” series of books. Josh-Posh and his sister, Little Hug-Me find a dove with a broken wing and bring her home. Pepe soon makes friends with Peppito, and the sparrows. The garden is filled with birds and little chicks. There is much chirping and joy . The children learn that everything that God creates has a purpose.

Josh-Posh, Little Hug-Me & The Butterfly

This is one of the stories from, "Created for a Purpose" series of books. Josh-Posh and his sister, Little Hug-Me stumble upon Crazy-Caterpillar. The children shrink in size after sipping honey from the red flowers. Crazy-Caterpillar spins the children into its shell. The children find themselves trapped within the shell and watch with amazement as Crazy-Caterpillar transforms into a beautiful Butterfly. Crazy-Caterpillar tells the children not to call anyone ugly. Everyone is a work in progress with a unique purpose.

Josh-Posh, Little Hug-Me & The Fireflies

This is one of the stories from the "Created for a Purpose" series. Josh-Posh and his sister, Little Hug-Me, meet the Chief of the fireflies. Fire-Beetle introduces the children to the twelve noble fireflies of Gach'lilit – the Gotcha Lit The World Tribe. Fireflies from all across the world descend upon the carrot field. Boo-Boo-Blooper-Bully traps the fireflies into jars. The children help release the fireflies. The King of the fireflies visits them. Boo-Boo-Blooper-Bully transforms into Super-Nice-Buddy.

Ruth a Journey to faith

Ruth is a Princess of Moab. Naomi her mother-in-law from Israel shares with her the love of Yahweh. Naomi's God and people become Ruth's God and people. As Ruth learns to trust in the one true God of Creation, the Lord honors her faithfulness. Her journey to faith in Yahweh is rewarded. and she becomes an ancestor to kings!

Tisp the Little Sparrow

"I am Tsip a little slip of a sparrow,
A sparrow ordinary,
with help from above,
I am Sparrow extraordinary"

Tsip, a rejected fledgling, is saved by the Baker’s daughter and raised by Grandma Sparrow. Tsip is lost and is rescued by Gab. Tsip finds a glowing Wonder Seed. Comforted by Bead, she cares for others and meets the God who cares for the sparrows. Tsip , Bead and Asher, save the sparrows of the Steeple Colony from Abaddon.
